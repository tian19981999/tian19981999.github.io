Joey Tianyi Zhou

I am a senior scientist, investigator with the A*STAR Centre for Frontier AI Research (CFAR), Singapore. I am also holding an appointment as an Adjunct faculty (Assco. Prof.) at the National University of Singapore (NUS).
I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Nanyang Technological University (NTU), SG, in 2015. I had been a snr. research engineer at Sony US Research Center, San Jose, USA before.

My research focus is on how to enhance Machine Learning Efficiency and Robustness. An ideal machine learning model shall : (1) be resource-efficient for both inference and training, including computational-efficient and data-efficient; (2)be safe to various perturbations, distribution shifts, and adversarial attacks.
